About IRYM 0.1

IRYM 0.1 is part of my ongoing personal AI and development project series — designed and built entirely by me, the creator. This version focuses on intelligent image design, enabling you to bring your vision to life effortlessly.

With IRYM, you can create a free account and log in to access powerful tools built with advanced AI. You can start by generating a fast design from a simple idea or prompt. Once created, you have the option to edit the design using our smart AI editor — or take full control using the Edit Room for advanced custom modifications.

IRYM also allows you to create full projects, where you can manage and design multiple images under one theme. Each image in your project can be fine-tuned using AI tools or manually refined to your liking.

Whether you’re designing for fun, creating a professional portfolio, or experimenting with generative art, IRYM gives you the tools to imagine and execute — fast, smart, and beautifully.
